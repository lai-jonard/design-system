la-info-hint Properties

la-info-hint

Inline contextual help — a small info-icon affordance next to a term, stat, or control that reveals a light tooltip with more explanation on hover or focus. Use to unpack jargon (DD Readiness, Company manual), explain metric definitions, or clarify non-obvious rules without taking space on the page. Pairs a ph-info icon with la-tooltip appearance="light", keeping the common case one component deep.
