la-switch

A binary on/off toggle. Wraps a native <input type="checkbox" role="switch"> so form submission and screen-reader semantics come free; visuals are a pill track with a sliding circular thumb, all token-driven. Use for settings, feature flags, and any stateful preference that flips instantly on click. For multi-select lists or form fields that need an indeterminate state, reach for la-checkbox instead.
