la-insight-list Properties

la-insight-list

Organises multiple la-insight-card instances into a scannable feed grouped by severity. Renders a labelled section for each non-empty group (urgent · proactive · resolved) with a count, shows the top N cards per group by default, and reveals the rest on demand via a "Show more" toggle. The list itself emits no events — la-insight-click and la-action bubble up from the child cards, so consumers can listen at the list level.
