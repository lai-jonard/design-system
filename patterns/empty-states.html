Empty states

What to show when a list, section, or page has no content yet. Always use la-empty-state — never a raw paragraph or blank space. Include a CTA when a direct next action exists.

Examples

No documents yet — primary CTA
Upload document
No founders added — secondary CTA
Add founder

Rules

  • Always use la-empty-state — never a raw paragraph or blank space.
  • Include a CTA when a direct next action exists.
  • For locked areas, use the locked variant with no CTA.
  • Never use "No results found" — use the noun: "No documents yet", "No founders added".

Copy

Inviting and actionable. Tell users what they'll gain, not what's missing.

Good "No documents yet. Upload your formation docs to get started." Avoid "Nothing here." / "No results found." / "This section is empty."
