Confirmation modal

Gate any hard-to-reverse action behind a two-button modal. Single heading, one-sentence body, exactly two footer buttons. Affirmative right, Cancel left — always.

Examples

Destructive confirmation — danger variant on the affirmative button
Open destructive confirmation

Alex Chen will be removed from the founders list. This cannot be undone.

Cancel Remove
Non-destructive confirmation — success variant on the affirmative button
Open non-destructive confirmation

This insight will be marked as resolved and removed from the active list.

Cancel Mark resolved

Rules

  • Single heading, one-sentence body, exactly two footer buttons.
  • Affirmative button always goes last (right). Cancel always goes first (left).
  • Destructive confirmations: affirmative = danger variant.
  • Non-destructive confirmations (mark resolved, send for signature): affirmative = primary or success variant.
  • Never more than two buttons — if three options exist, one belongs elsewhere in the UI.
